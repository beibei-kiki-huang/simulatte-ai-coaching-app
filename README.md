### AI-Powered Coffee Chat Coach for International Graduate Students

## The Problem

**80% of USC MSBA's 2025 cohort are international students** — yet coffee chats 
are the primary gateway to referrals, mentorship, and job opportunities in the US.

Survey of 48 MSBA students revealed:

| Pain Point | Data |
|---|---|
| Low small talk confidence (≤3/5) | **78%** of respondents |
| Low relationship-building confidence (≤3/5) | **72%** of respondents |
| Already using AI to prep for coffee chats | **70%** of respondents |
| Unsatisfied with AI prep (rated ≤3/5) | **50%** of those users |

Top complaints about existing AI tools: *"too generic," "not specific enough," 
"doesn't prepare for the social aspect."*

---

## The Solution

**SimuLatte** is a USC MSBA-specific AI coaching app with four core features:

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| ☕ Coffee Chat Simulator | Practice mock conversations with an AI coach |
| 💬 Small Talk Generator | Context-aware conversation starters |
| 🎯 Background-Tailored Questions | Questions customized to your chat partner's profile |
| ✉️ Follow-Up Draft | Personalized post-chat follow-up emails |

After each session, users receive a scored performance report across 4 dimensions: 
Preparation, Engagement, Professionalism, and Wrap-up.

---

## Research & Methodology

- **Primary research:** 48-respondent survey of USC MSBA D3 cohort
- **Secondary research:** USC Thrive international student data; industry benchmarks
- **Product design:** PRD with user stories + wireframe (Figma-style prototype)
- **Behavior change strategy:** Gamification (Rookie → Conversationalist → Connector), 
  streak rewards, faculty-led workshop integration

---

## Implementation Plan

**Phase 1 – Pilot:** Workshop demo + small group adoption  
**Phase 2 – Scale:** USC Career Services partnership + integration into GSBA 542  
**Phase 3 – Expansion:** Alumni participation + other graduate programs
